Toki Privacy Policy

Last updated: November 2025

1. Overview

Toki is a Chrome extension that helps users track browsing time and productivity. We respect your privacy and are committed to ensuring that all data stays securely on your device.

2. Data Collection

  • Toki does not collect or transmit any personal data to external servers.
  • All tracked data (like time spent per site, productivity scores, and settings) is stored locally in your browser using Chrome’s storage APIs.
  • No analytics or external tracking tools are used.

3. How Data Is Used

  • To display statistics about your browsing time and productivity.
  • To enable features like time limits, focus nudges, and streak tracking.
  • To remember your settings, tags, and ignored sites.

4. Permissions Explained

  • tabs: Detects the current website to track time spent.
  • idle: Pauses tracking when you’re inactive.
  • storage: Saves your data locally.
  • alarms: Handles periodic saves and daily resets.
  • webNavigation: Detects navigation between pages for accuracy.
  • notifications: Sends focus reminders or time limit alerts.
  • contextMenus: Adds quick tagging options on right-click.

5. Data Retention & Deletion

  • Your data remains on your device until you delete it.
  • You can reset or remove all tracking data anytime in the extension’s settings.
  • Uninstalling the extension permanently deletes all stored data.

6. Security

Because Toki stores everything locally and does not connect to any external servers, your data never leaves your browser. Chrome’s built-in sandboxing and storage security mechanisms keep your data safe.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time to reflect new features or legal requirements. Updates will always be available at this page.
